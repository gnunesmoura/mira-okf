# Spec-Driven Development

This area contains the templates, rules, and executable change packages for
Spec-Driven Development in the `tooling` bundle.

## Areas

- [`changes/`](changes/) - Scoped packages for approved and executed changes.
- [`templates/`](templates/) - Reusable package artifact templates.
- [`guides/`](guides/) - How people and agents work with SDD.
- [`policies/`](policies/) - Normative structure, lifecycle, and evidence rules.

Change packages are the normative source for the scope and execution state of
the change they describe. Durable product capabilities remain in
[`../product/features/`](../product/features/), and reusable technical
contracts remain in [`../architecture/`](../architecture/).
