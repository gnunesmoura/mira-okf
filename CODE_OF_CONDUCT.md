# Code of Conduct

The `tooling` community is expected to be respectful, constructive, and
inclusive. Participants must:

- communicate professionally and assume good faith;
- welcome different backgrounds, experience levels, and viewpoints;
- give actionable feedback about ideas and work, not personal attacks; and
- respect privacy and avoid harassment, discrimination, threats, or unwanted
  contact.

Conduct concerns should be reported privately to `gnunes.moura@gmail.com` with
the subject `[okf-tooling][CONDUCT]`. `gnunesmoura` is the maintainer and the
responsible authority for conduct and governance decisions during alpha.
No moderation response or resolution SLA is provided.
